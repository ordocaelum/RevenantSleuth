## Blocking & Camera
This is a functional scene — it exists to establish the operation's parameters before the infiltration begins. Keep it tight (4–6 shots, minimal coverage). The emotion is in the subtext: Kane tries to apply rules to an unpredictable situation; Evelyn accepts the rules and mentally notes the exits from the rules.

The key blocking note: when Kane says "if anything feels wrong, you leave," Evelyn does not nod quickly or reassuringly. She takes a beat — as if deciding whether to say something — and then gives a single deliberate nod. This one beat tells us she has already gamed out several scenarios Kane hasn't mentioned.

## Dialogue (adapted — consistent, not exact)
> KANE: Objective is information. Get in, observe, get back out. You see anything worth documenting, document it. If anything feels wrong — anything at all — say the word and you leave.

> EVELYN: *(adjusting the mic)* And if leaving isn't an option?

> KANE: Then you stall. And I burn the place down if I have to.

> *(She holds his gaze for a beat.)*

> EVELYN: Understood.

> KANE: *(V.O.)* We planned it the way you plan a fire — assume it will spread.
