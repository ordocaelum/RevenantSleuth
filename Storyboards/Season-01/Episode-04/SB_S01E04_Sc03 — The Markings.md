## Blocking & Camera
This is a scene in two physical spaces simultaneously: the rooftop (where we stay visually) and the club interior (which we experience only through Evelyn's voice). The camera does not go inside the club yet — that anticipation is the scene's engine.

Kane's movement on the rooftop should be contained and purposeful. He doesn't pace; he repositions. When the comms cut, he goes still. When they return, he moves to a better position.

The scene ends before Voss's voice enters the comms — that is the next scene's revelation.

## Dialogue (adapted — consistent, not exact)
> EVELYN: *(earpiece)* Kane. I'm seeing markings. On the pillars, the door frames. Etched into the décor. Not just art. They look... archaic.

> KANE: Describe them.

> EVELYN: Curved lines. Repeating shapes. Like a language, but nothing I recognize.

> KANE: Take a photo if you can.

> EVELYN: I'm at the bar and—

> *(Static.)*

> KANE: Evelyn? Evelyn, talk to me.

> EVELYN: *(returning, lower)* A bouncer just approached. The owner wants to see me.

> KANE: Decline.

> *(Pause.)*

> EVELYN: His men aren't asking.

> KANE: Stall. Keep him talking. I'm watching.

> KANE (V.O.): I shifted position, trying to see anything — movement near the back door, a shadow at the lit window.

> KANE (V.O.): On my way, I told myself. Even though I wasn't sure yet where "way" was.
