# SB_S01E01_Sc01 — December Briefing
- Source: The Story, Chapter 1 "Crimson Veil" — bullpen body-cam viewing
- Location: INT. HOMICIDE BULLPEN — NIGHT
- Characters: Kane (narrator), assembled detectives/officers (background), unnamed sergeant (off-screen voice)
- Beat summary: Kane stands in the dimly lit bullpen as the room watches body-cam footage on monitors. The footage captures a family massacre and something impossible climbing out of a sedan. ## Blocking & Camera
Kane stands center-left, slightly behind the main cluster of watchers — he is present but separate. He doesn't lean in like the others. The camera-at-the-monitors is Kane's eye-level.

When the footage ends, Kane is the first to stop looking at the screen. He has already moved to the next question.

The room's geometry should reinforce isolation: Kane near the back wall, the crowd clustered near the screens, a physical gap between him and the group.
