# SB_S01E01_Sc04 — The City Already Knows
- Source: The Story, Chapter 1 "Crimson Veil" — drive through city (ambient infection signs)
- Location: EXT. CITY STREETS — NIGHT / INT. CAR — NIGHT

## Blocking & Camera
The drive is a series of connected POV/medium shots — Kane's perspective on the city, and the city's return gaze. The people he sees are not characters; they are symptoms.

He never turns to look at them directly. He watches in the periphery, from the windshield and the side windows, the way you watch things you've learned to notice without acknowledging.

When he parks, we see the Velvet Abyss for the first time — not close, not heroic. Just a neon wound at the end of the block.

## Dialogue (adapted — consistent, not exact)
> KANE (V.O.): I drove without music. Just engine noise and everything I'd seen grinding behind my eyes.

> KANE (V.O.): The drug wasn't a rumor. It was already in the bloodstream of the city. I could see it in the streets — if I knew what to look for. The eyes that were too wide. The hands that didn't stop moving. The man standing in the road like he was waiting for something to tell him where to go.

> KANE (V.O.): By the time I parked, I already had a name in my head.

> KANE (V.O.): The Velvet Abyss.
