## Blocking & Camera
The alley is a corridor — Kane moves along one wall, using the environment as cover. The camera stays tight to him during the approach. The two bouncers are established with wide shots early, then the camera stays with Kane at ground level.

The bass thumping through the bricks should be expressed as a physical sensation — Kane's body responds to each beat slightly. Not the obedience reflex — he's not contaminated. Just the physical reality of bass resonating through brick and into the alley.
