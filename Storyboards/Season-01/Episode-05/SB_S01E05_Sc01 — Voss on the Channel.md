# SB_S01E05_Sc01 — Voss on the Channel
- Source: The Story, Chapter 3 "Into the Abyss" — Voss seizes the comms channel
- Location: EXT. ROOFTOP — NIGHT (Kane) / INT. VELVET ABYSS UPPER FLOOR (Voss — heard, not seen)
- Characters: Kane (rooftop), Voss (voice only)
- Beat summary: Evelyn's comms goes dark. When it returns, it isn't Evelyn. It's Voss — smooth, amused, in possession of Kane's private channel. He plays host while Kane plays the man trying not to show how exposed he is. "Did you think you were the one hunting?" The wind dies. Not eases — dies. Kane's hair rises. He is not in control of this moment.

## Blocking & Camera
Kane does not move dramatically during this scene — he is trapped by the channel, pinned by the revelation that his comms have been compromised from the beginning. His physical stillness is the blocking. His tension is expressed entirely through small controlled movements: jaw clench, hand grip on the rooftop parapet, forced breathing.

The lit upper window should hold in frame when Voss speaks — the audience has been tracking it for two scenes and now it pays off. Voss is behind that window. He is looking at Kane even as Kane looks at him.

The scene should feel like a chess move revealed: Kane thought he was watching the board; the board was watching him.

## Dialogue (adapted — consistent, not exact)
> *(The earpiece clicks. Different voice.)*

> VOSS: Detective Kane.

> KANE: Who is this?

> VOSS: You can call me Voss. I believe this channel belongs to me tonight.

> KANE: Get off my channel.

> VOSS: *(amused)* I believe, in fact, this channel belongs to me tonight. Along with a few other things.

> KANE: Where is she?

> VOSS: She's safe. For now. *(pause)* But I'm curious, detective — did you think you were the one hunting?

> KANE: Let her go.

> VOSS: You're standing on a rooftop. In December. Watching a building you can't enter. And you think this is a standoff.

> *(A soft chuckle.)*

> VOSS: Come inside, detective. The bass is wonderful.

> *(Comms cuts.)*

## Continuity Flags
- Voss says "the bass is wonderful" — this is NOT a coincidence. He knows Kane has been hearing the bass on the rooftop. He may know about Evelyn's sensitivity. He is telling Kane that the bass is a feature, not a symptom.
- REVEAL DISCIPLINE: Do not indicate that the bass command is Voss's creation. He is a node in the system. The bass belongs to the Lich. Voss administers it.
- "Did you think you were the one hunting?" — this question should become a thematic echo. When Kane achieves clarity in Ch.9–10, he will be able to answer it definitively. For now, he has no answer.
- Voss's restraint: he has Kane on the rooftop, exposed, Evelyn inside. He could end this. He doesn't. Per Character Bible: "lets Kane go against his preference." Show the restraint — show Voss not escalating when he could. Make it feel odd.
