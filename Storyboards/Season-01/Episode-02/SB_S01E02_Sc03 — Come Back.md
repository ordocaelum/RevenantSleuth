## Blocking & Camera
This scene is almost entirely in the dark — the only light is Kane's flashlight, which is the scene's editorial tool. What the beam finds is what we know. What it misses stays hidden.

The head rolling: do NOT cut away. The camera stays on the floor as it rolls. The viewer watches it reach the boot. This is the scene's most important commitment: we are not sparing anyone here.

After finding the note, Kane does NOT panic, does NOT run, does NOT call for backup immediately. He stands with the note until the letters stop being letters and become a voice. Then he backs out carefully. The restraint is the horror.
