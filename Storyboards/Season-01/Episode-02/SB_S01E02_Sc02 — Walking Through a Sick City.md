# SB_S01E02_Sc02 — Walking Through a Sick City
- Source: The Story, Chapter 1 "Crimson Veil" — walk home through the infected city
- Location: EXT. CITY STREETS — NIGHT
- Characters: Kane (walking)
- Beat summary: Kane walks home. The city is already bleeding. He notices what the job has trained him to see — symptoms in the street, the drug's reach wider than one club. The neon looks like bruises now. He files it and keeps walking, collar up, hands in pockets, the whole weight of what DeMarco said riding inside him.

## Blocking & Camera
This is a walking scene — continuous movement through a living city that is quietly wrong. No single event happens; instead, the environment accumulates. The rhythm should be: two beats of observation, one beat of Kane moving through, two beats of observation, etc.

The final approach to the building is a slow push-in from behind — Kane small against the building, the hallway light flickering through the lobby glass. It looks like home. It is not about to behave like home.
