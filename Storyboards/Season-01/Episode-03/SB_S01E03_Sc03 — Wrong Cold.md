# SB_S01E03_Sc03 — Wrong Cold
- Source: The Story, Chapter 2 "Alliance Forged in Darkness" — the alley; the wrong-cold homeless man
- Location: EXT. ALLEY (adjacent to jazz bar) — NIGHT
- Characters: Kane, Evelyn (distant), Wrong-Cold Homeless Man
- Beat summary: Kane walks down the block after leaving the bar. A figure peels out of a doorway — a homeless man, or something wearing the shape of one. He asks for change. When his fingers brush Kane's, the cold is wrong. Not winter cold. Meat-freezer cold. And then Kane sees the branching veins at his throat. "Not all monsters wear fangs." The man steps back into shadow and is gone.

## Blocking & Camera
The homeless man appears from a doorway that Kane passes — not directly in his path, but to his side. Kane should not turn all the way to face him until the branching veins are visible. The scene is staged so that the veins are only visible when Kane tilts his look downward — they are on the lower throat, not immediately visible.

When the man steps back into shadow, he does not "disappear" with any VFX — the shadow simply takes him. The empty doorway holds for one beat. The absence is the horror, not any dramatic effect.

## Dialogue (adapted — consistent, not exact)
> HOMELESS MAN: *(rasping)* Spare something?

> *(Kane hands him bills without breaking stride. Their fingers brush.)*

> KANE: *(to himself, stopping)* ...

> HOMELESS MAN: *(smiling, gums showing)* Thanks.

> *(Kane looks down. Sees the throat.)*

> HOMELESS MAN: *(quiet, deliberate)* Be careful, detective. Not all monsters wear fangs.

> *(He steps back. Gone.)*

> KANE (V.O.): I stood there a moment too long. Staring at nothing. Feeling the city's breath on the back of my neck. Then I kept walking. Because the note had given me a direction. And Evelyn had just given me a reason to follow it.

- When he steps back: the doorway shadow is genuine shadow — no emissive, no effect. Just absence of light.
