# SB_S02E01_Sc03 — Finding Evelyn
- Source: The Story, Chapter 4 "Back Door Gospel" — finding Evelyn in the red-lamp room
- Location: INT. VELVET ABYSS RED-LAMP ROOM — NIGHT
- Characters: Kane, Evelyn Sinclair
- Beat summary: Beyond the stitched-mouth attacker, Kane finds a smaller room off the main basement — a red-lamp room, separate from the factory floor. Evelyn is here, unharmed but shaken. She found what she went in looking for. She's writing in a small notebook. She has photographs. She's been watching the room adjacent to this one — a room she didn't enter. She tells Kane about the mirror. He tells her they need to leave. She disagrees about the order of operations.

## Blocking & Camera
The red-lamp room is a chamber of calm inside a very violent space. The contrast should be felt. The lighting is warm but wrong — red is warning, not comfort.

Both characters are competent. Evelyn is not scared — she is operating. Kane's concern for her is not panic, it is the controlled assessment of a professional who cares about someone.

The key beat is Evelyn's line about not looking directly into the mirror. She gives no further explanation. Kane doesn't press. Both actors should hold the silence that follows for a beat.

## Dialogue (adapted — consistent, not exact)
> KANE: You alright?

> EVELYN: Functional. Sit down.

> EVELYN: *(spreading photos)* I had about twelve minutes in the club before they got suspicious. Used eight of them productively.

> KANE: The factory floor?

> EVELYN: Got photographs. Got a count — fifteen tables, maybe twelve occupied tonight. The fridge is a pharmaceutical-grade cold storage unit. You've seen those before.

> KANE: I've seen what's in them.

> EVELYN: *(pointing to a photo)* There's a room. Off the main floor, separate. I didn't go in. There's a mirror inside. Black frame, big. The markings on the frame are the same as on the corridor walls.

> KANE: How close did you get?

> EVELYN: Close enough to photograph the frame. I didn't touch it. I didn't look directly into it.

> KANE: Why not?

> *(A beat.)*

> EVELYN: Didn't feel like a good idea. *(collecting photos)* We need that room documented.

> KANE: We need to be outside first.

> EVELYN: You check the corridor. I'll meet you.
