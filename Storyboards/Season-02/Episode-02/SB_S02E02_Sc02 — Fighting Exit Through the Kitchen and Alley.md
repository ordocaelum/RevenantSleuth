- Source: The Story, Chapter 4 "Back Door Gospel" — the fighting exit through the kitchen/alley
- Location: INT. VELVET ABYSS KITCHEN / EXT. VELVET ABYSS REAR ALLEY — NIGHT
- Characters: Kane, Evelyn Sinclair, kitchen/security staff x2–3
- Beat summary: Kane and Evelyn move through the service kitchen toward the alley exit. They're not alone. The bass is picking up — the club above them is mid-set, the pulse more insistent. The fight is fast and brutal; they don't fight like men protecting their employer — they fight like something is operating them. Kane and Evelyn make the alley.

## Blocking & Camera
Fast cuts in the kitchen fight — match the pace of the bass. The key VFX beat is shot 11: the workers pausing simultaneously when the bass shifts. Keep that shot clear and held for 2 beats so the audience registers it as intentional.

Evelyn fighting: she is efficient and economical — no hesitation, no wide swings. Former IA instinct. She reads the threat and closes it.

The alley exit is the breathing room after the violence — the cold air, the familiar space from Sc01, the glow of the door getting smaller.
