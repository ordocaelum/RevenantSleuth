- Characters: Kane, Rook Alvarez, (Sinclair — elsewhere, connected by phone)
- Beat summary: The Velvet Abyss has been shut — whether by the department, by Voss's decision, or by events, it is dark. Kane and Rook move through it. Among them, something that Rook translates as a title or a name: "The Keeper of the Cold." Rook is the one who reads it; Kane is the one who understands what it means.
