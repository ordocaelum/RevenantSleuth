- Location: INT. KANE'S APARTMENT — DAWN
- Characters: Kane (alone)
- Beat summary: Series finale. The city is quieter. Not clean. Kane's apartment — dawn light for the first time. He sits at the case box. The files are in order. He writes a last entry. Then he finds one final note on the windowsill. No handwriting this time — just the Polaroid from S02E06, placed back. The one of him and Max. Someone returned it. The back is blank now — "You still grab sleeves" has been erased. What that means, Kane does not say.
